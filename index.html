Appetizer — Fresh Garden Salad

View
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cucumber
  • Dressing
  1. Wash your leafy greens and dry them completely using a salad spinner or by laying them on a clean towel.
  2. Slice, chop, or dice your additional vegetables.
  3. In a small jar or bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice/vinegar, salt, pepper, and optional honey until combined.
  4. Place the dried, torn, or chopped lettuce into a large bowl.
  5. Just before serving, pour the dressing over the salad and toss gently to coat everything lightly.
  6. Top with cheeses, nuts, or croutons for extra crunch and flavor.

Main Course — Garlic Butter Chicken

View
  • Chicken breast
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Salt & pepper
  1. Pat 1.5 lbs of chicken breasts dry with paper towels to ensure a good sear.
  2. In a large skillet, melt 1 tbsp of butter with 1 tbsp of olive oil over medium-high heat.
  3. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside on a plate.
  4. Reduce the heat to medium.
  5. Pour in 1/2 cup of chicken broth. Use a wooden spoon to scrape up all the browned bits.
  6. Let the sauce simmer for 2-3 minutes to reduce slightly.
  7. Return the chicken and any resting juices back to the skillet.

Drink — Lemon Iced Tea

View
  • Tea
  • Lemon
  • Sugar
  • Ice
  1. Bring 4 cups of water to a rolling boil in a saucepan or kettle.
  2. Remove the water from the heat.
  3. Remove the tea bags or strain the leaves.
  4. Pour the hot tea concentrate into your pitcher and add 4 cups of cold water.
  5. Let the tea sit until it reaches room temperature, then place it in the refrigerator until thoroughly chilled.
  6. Just before serving, stir in fresh lemon juice, lemon slices, and mint leaves.
  7. Fill glasses with ice cubes and pour the tea over them.
